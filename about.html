About

Friday 24 & Saturday 25 June 2022 | Finstock Playing Field | Free Entry | Card Payments | Craft Beers & Gin Bar | Food Throughout

Why? Over £5000 raised so far.

Finstock Festival is run by Finstock Community Projects, an umbrella fundraising organisation for other organisations in the village. Each year we donate money raised at the festival to some of these fantastic organisations, such as the School and Village Shop. For more information about the money raised and where it's gone, please see the Finstock Community Projects website.
