The Music

Two nights of quality local music...

Friday 24 June, from 7:30pm

Growler

Headlining Friday at Finstock Festival again after their tremendous set at the previous festival - we're excited to welcome them back! Growler is a five-piece Oxfordshire-based covers band playing a mix of classic rock and punk songs in their own, unique way.

Studded Rose

Formerly the Eron Roberts Band who have played at previous festivals, Studden Rose debut with their mix of rock classics from AC/DC to Metallica!

Saturday 25 June
from 2pm

Buskers in the bar

from 5pm

Warren James and the Hot Band

Headlining Saturday night, variety entertainer and impressionist Guitar, banjo and ukulele virtuoso Warren James has no equal!

Midlife Crisis

Playing after our headliners to keep you dancing into the night, Midlife Crisis play classic covers from the likes of The Jam, the Rolling Stones, and many more.

The Dead Silence

Kicking off our fantastic evening of music, it's The Dead Silence making their debut appearance at the festival.
